The Opportunity

Every creative seeks to become masters of their craft. Always seeking to learn.

Many lack the tools to effectively share the knowledge that helps.

The Problem

The lack of

CULTURE

Creative communities are broken, they are too focused on vanity metrics. If the cultured is defined as such then who cares to critique.

ACCESSIBILITY

It's difficult to share thoughts and critiques on work shared in existing communities and it takes too much time.

SUPPORT

Most creatives don't receive constructive critiques from the people who can help make them better.

Let's look at one of the popular design community today

The culture is defined by likes, views and followers. A great platform for showcasing but not for constructive critiques.

Every image is cropped 400x300. This makes it difficult to give constructive critiques given the lack of context.

If a constructive critique is perceived as lengthy and difficult then it's understandably hard to get critiques.

that brings us to

Our Solution

Create the tools and design the culture that facilitates constructive critiques.

Every creative has their own insights to share about the work of their peers.

ANNOTATIONS

Critiques are given through annotations and because of this has more context. As a result, critiques can be short but constructive.

KARMA

Karma is obtained through constructive critiques that are determined by your peers. Karma serves as your reputation. Good Karma allows user to submit designs and reqest critiques.

REQUESTS

Access to accomplished creatives have always been a challenge in communities. Hunie aims to allow designers such access through karma and requests.

 

OPPORTUNITIES

Hunie aims to attract amazing companies to connect them to the right talent including designers, photographers, film-makers, motion designers, etc.

MONETIZATION STRATEGY

Subscription for Designers

Designers subscribe to a pro account for additional features and benefit.

Transactional Fees

Hunie will charge transaction fee on design projects designers find on Hunie.

Subscription for Recruiters

Employers and recruiters will subscribe to Hunie to find talent.

An active and engaged community based on learning and constructive critiques will attract creative talent across the world. Hunie will match the right talent within the community with the right companies.

User Acquisition

Schools will share with their students while mentors will share with their following.

Schools

Design schools will be given their own private groups for their students to connect with alumni through critiques.

Mentors
